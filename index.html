In my time in college, one thing I have discovered is what way I prefer to learn. Everyone has their own unique way of learning and without knowing your preferred learning style, then your studying and in-class activity's are going to be negatively impacted. This website is designed to give you all the learning tips you need to improve your journey in whatever level education you reside in.

Before you begin to work on anything, you first must organise yourself. Make sure everything you need is neatly in arms reach of you and anything you don't need is out of sight. Keep a clear headspace before beginning.

Use an app on your phone or a spare notepad to write down general notes like what goals you plan to reach today. planning out your goals may help you keep track and make learning easier for you.

Keep distractions away from you while in class. Instead, try take notes of discussions in class that might prove helpful when returning to the topic to study it.

When revising notes, this time, go over what you think is helpful with a highlighter. That way key notes are easy to see and even easier to remember

Its good to have friends in your classes, that way, if you ever miss class you have someone that can catch you up on anything you missed. Dont be shy!

If you are ever confused or uncertain about a topic you are learning, remember that you can always reach out to your teacher/Lecturer for help. Its what they are there for.

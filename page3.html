Stress

Everyone has felt stressed at least once in their life. Especially when leading up to a big exam day, it can be overwhelming, and when that happens its important to know how to handle it and to not let it hinder your learning and studying.

Here are some tips to help you avoid stress.
  • Takes breaks consistently and distract yourself with a hobby
  • Take notes of what you need to revise and track it as you go
  • Ask for help if you're feeling lost
  • Manage your time wisely
  • Avoid procrastination at all costs
  • Go for a walk or your preferred form of exercise to free up your mind
  • Keep a consistently good slepe schedule
  • Understand what is stressing you out and how you can prevent it.
  • Dont let the stress take over- always reach out for help if you need it

    • Time Management

    Having good time management is essential when it comes to any of your academic work. You need to know how to divide your time up wisely and put it to good use. Do not forget to make time for the things you love though, have equal time for both learning and relaxing to avoid stressing yourself out and feeling burnt out

    Here are some tips that can help you manage your time wisely
  • Create a schedule to use as a visual aid of what you plan to do today
  • Avoid procrastination and cut anything out that may lead to it
  • identify what part of the day you are most productive
  • Stick to learning only one topic at a given time
  • Portion your breaks appropriately
  • Set realistic time limits
  • Prioritise your goals and pick them wisely
  • Keep your surrounding organised to keep your mind organised
  • Dont put half-efforts when studying/learning. Go all in or all out
